SFDATrack: Generalized Source-Free Domain Adaptive Tracking Under Adverse Weather Conditions
Siyuan Yao, Ziqi Wang, Ruiqi Yu, Junqi Huang, Wenqi Ren, Xiaochun Cao2026-07-01 (submitted 2026-06-30)
This paper addresses the challenge of maintaining reliable visual tracking in adverse weather conditions (fog, rain, snow, haze) without access to the original source-domain training data. The proposed Source-Free Domain Adaptive Tracking (SFDA-Track) framework operates exclusively on target-domain footage. The architecture features a mean-teacher backbone equipped with Dual Interactive Mamba layers that extract weather-resilient features through bidirectional selective scanning. A hyperspherical prototype projection mechanism maps these features into a shared latent space, ensuring robust adaptability across diverse climate conditions. This approach eliminates the need for source data while achieving competitive tracking performance in degraded visual environments.
本文解决了在恶劣天气条件（雾、雨、雪、霾）下保持可靠视觉跟踪的挑战，且无需访问原始源域训练数据。所提出的无源域自适应跟踪（SFDA-Track）框架完全基于目标域数据进行自适应。架构采用配备双重交互式Mamba层的均值教师骨干网络，通过双向选择性扫描提取抗天气特征。超球原型投影机制将特征映射到共享潜在空间，确保在多种气候条件下的鲁棒自适应能力。该方法无需源域数据即可在退化视觉环境中实现有竞争力的跟踪性能。

FalconTrack: Photorealistic Auto-Labeled Perception and Physics-Aware Vision-Based Aerial Tracking
Yan Miao, Karteek Gandiboyina, Noah Giles, Hideki Okamoto, Bardh Hoxha, Georgios Fainekos, Sayan Mitra2026-06-29
This paper presents an integrated system for aerial visual tracking that addresses the critical challenge of generating large-scale annotated datasets without manual labeling. The approach leverages a photorealistic, configurable simulation environment to automatically produce training annotations. An automated pipeline uses neural rendering to isolate objects from short video clips and composite them onto random backgrounds, rapidly generating thousands of annotated images. A detection model trained in simulation is then combined with motion prediction through a physics-aware filtering architecture for robust real-world tracking. The system achieved exceptional precision when deployed on real drones without prior real-world training, and a physical drone successfully tracked targets in real-time flight, surpassing conventional visual tracking approaches.
本文提出了一个用于空中视觉跟踪的集成系统，解决了无需手动标注生成大规模标注数据集的关键挑战。该方法利用逼真可配置的仿真环境自动生成训练标注。自动化流水线使用神经渲染从短视频片段中隔离目标并将其合成到随机背景上，快速生成数千张标注图像。在仿真中训练的检测模型随后通过物理感知滤波架构与运动预测结合，实现鲁棒的真实世界跟踪。该系统在真实无人机上部署时无需预训练即达到高精度，物理无人机在实时飞行中成功跟踪目标，超越了传统视觉跟踪方法。
  • Neural rendering-based auto-labeling pipeline / 基于神经渲染的自动标注流水线
  • Physics-aware motion filtering / 物理感知运动滤波
  • Sim-to-real transfer / 仿真到真实的迁移
Multi-Object Tracking (MOT) / 多目标跟踪
HieDG: A Hierarchical Discrete Geometry-Guided Framework for Multi-Animal Tracking (ECCV 2026)
Chenxun Deng, Zhongde Zhang, Ye Yuan, Chengyang Zhang, Yifan Zhang, Bohao Chen, Hongying Yan, Hang Zhou, Hua Han, Xi Chen2026-07-01
Tracking multiple animals is essential for wildlife observation but remains challenging due to similar appearances and erratic movements. Current methods struggle with the instability of continuous spatial representations. HieDG introduces a novel two-stage residual codebook that converts spatial cues (position, velocity) into structured, stable discrete tokens. These discrete representations are merged with visual features to enhance identity matching across frames. The model achieved top results on specialized wildlife tracking datasets and demonstrated strong generalization on general multi-object tracking benchmarks. This work has been accepted to ECCV 2026.
多动物跟踪对野生动物观察至关重要，但由于外观相似和运动不规则，仍然具有挑战性。当前方法在连续空间表示的不稳定性方面表现不佳。HieDG引入了新颖的两阶段残差码本，将空间线索（位置、速度）转换为结构化、稳定的离散token。这些离散表示与视觉特征融合以增强跨帧身份匹配。该模型在专业野生动物跟踪数据集上取得了顶级结果，并在通用多目标跟踪基准上展示了强泛化能力。该工作已被ECCV 2026接收。
  • Two-stage residual codebook for spatial tokenization / 两阶段残差码本用于空间离散化
  • Hierarchical discrete geometry guidance / 层次化离散几何引导
PS-MOT: Cultivating Instance Awareness from Point Seeds for Multi-Object Tracking
Kai Luo, Fei Teng, Mengfei Duan, Wanjun Jia, Xu Wang, Hao Shi, Kunyu Peng, Zhiyong Li, Kailun Yang2026-06-29
This paper presents a cost-effective approach to multi-object tracking using only point annotations (center markers) instead of full bounding boxes. The system, called PS-Track, shifts emphasis from physical perimeters to central topology. To address the ambiguity of point-only supervision, the authors introduce three key innovations: (1) Temporal-Feedback Prompting converts basic point markers into stable pseudo-labels using motion predictions and negative spatial hints; (2) Point-Excited Wavelet Attention leverages semantic connections to trigger high-frequency details and predict object boundaries; (3) Uncertainty-Guided Gaussian Learning treats pseudo-labels as probability distributions to dynamically adjust training weights. Evaluations across multiple datasets demonstrate new state-of-the-art results for point-supervised tracking.
本文提出了一种经济高效的多目标跟踪方法，仅使用点标注（中心标记）而非完整边界框。该系统称为PS-Track，将重点从物理边界转向中心拓扑。为解决纯点监督的模糊性问题，作者引入三项关键创新：（1）时序反馈提示使用运动预测和负空间提示将基本点标记转换为稳定的伪标签；（2）点激励小波注意力利用语义连接触发高频细节并预测对象边界；（3）不确定性引导高斯学习将伪标签视为概率分布以动态调整训练权重。在多个数据集上的评估展示了点监督跟踪的新SOTA结果。
  • Temporal-Feedback Prompting / 时序反馈提示
  • Point-Excited Wavelet Attention / 点激励小波注意力
  • Uncertainty-Guided Gaussian Learning / 不确定性引导高斯学习
CylindTrack: Depth-Aware Cylindrical Motion Modeling for Panoramic Multi-Object Tracking
Buyin Deng, Kai Luo, Lingxin Huang, Xinqi Liu, Fei Cheng, Hang Zheng, Liming Yin, Kailun Yang2026-06-29
Panoramic cameras provide 360° views for autonomous systems but introduce unique challenges for multi-object tracking. The circular distortion of the horizontal axis breaks standard motion models and ruins overlap-based matching at image boundaries. CylindTrack addresses these issues with three depth-aware modules: (1) DTM upgrades single-frame depth estimates into smoothed trajectory states; (2) SSTC boosts observation reliability by merging temporal mixing with geometry-focused attention; (3) TCMM translates lateral motion into a continuous angular space, ensuring smooth predictions across the panoramic field of view. This work represents one of the first dedicated solutions for panoramic multi-object tracking.
全景相机为自主系统提供360°视角，但给多目标跟踪带来独特挑战。水平轴的环形畸变破坏了标准运动模型，并在图像边界处破坏了基于重叠的匹配。CylindTrack通过三个深度感知模块解决这些问题：（1）DTM将单帧深度估计升级为平滑的轨迹状态；（2）SSTC通过融合时序混合与几何聚焦注意力提升观测可靠性；（3）TCMM将横向运动转换为连续角度空间，确保全景视场中平滑的预测。该工作是全景多目标跟踪的首批专用解决方案之一。
  • Depth-aware Trajectory Modeling (DTM) / 深度感知轨迹建模
  • Spatio-Temporal Channel mixing (SSTC) / 时空通道混合
  • Toroidal Continuous Motion Mapping (TCMM) / 环形连续运动映射
Novel Architectures & State Space Models / 新型架构与状态空间模型
SUMO: Segment and Track Any Motion with Nonlinear State Space Models
Kexin Tian, Sixu Li, Keshu Wu, Yang Zhou, Zhengzhong Tu2026-06-29
SUMO presents a training-free, zero-shot approach to improving object tracking and segmentation of moving objects. Traditional tracking methods rely heavily on visual cues and fail when objects exhibit complex, non-linear real-world movements. SUMO merges non-linear dynamics from robotics with image-based segmentation. The system introduces a non-linear State Space Model (SSM) to capture complex object motions that linear models cannot represent. A Selective Unscented Filter provides precise state estimation, using unified scoring and smooth integration of multiple predictions to determine the most likely object state over time. A frame retention selector evaluates the trustworthiness of stored templates, yielding superior tracking results without any task-specific training.
SUMO提出了一种无需训练、零样本的方法来改善运动对象的跟踪和分割。传统跟踪方法严重依赖视觉线索，当对象表现出复杂的非线性真实世界运动时会失败。SUMO将机器人学中的非线性动力学与基于图像的分割相结合。系统引入非线性状态空间模型（SSM）来捕获线性模型无法表示的复杂对象运动。选择性无迹滤波器提供精确的状态估计，使用统一评分和多预测的平滑积分来确定最可能的对象状态。帧保留选择器评估存储模板的可信度，在无需任何任务特定训练的情况下产生优越的跟踪结果。
  • Nonlinear State Space Model / 非线性状态空间模型
  • Selective Unscented Filter / 选择性无迹滤波器
  • Frame retention selector / 帧保留选择器
Generic Object Tracking & Perceptual Intelligence / 通用目标跟踪与感知智能
Rethinking Generic Object Tracking Toward Human-Level Perceptual Intelligence
Shih-Fang Chen2026-07-01
This paper takes a reflective look at the fundamental goals of generic object tracking, drawing parallels between biological vision systems and artificial tracking algorithms. Biological sight maintains continuous environmental understanding by blending past knowledge with current visual input. Current artificial systems, initialized with a target in the first frame, struggle when objects undergo drastic appearance changes, face competing visual distractions, or belong to previously unseen classes. The author proposes multiple techniques to bridge the gap between artificial and biological vision, focusing on improving target discrimination, adaptive model updating, and spatial reasoning capabilities. This work provides a thought-provoking perspective on the future direction of tracking research toward human-level perceptual intelligence.
本文对通用目标跟踪的基本目标进行了反思性审视，在生物视觉系统和人工跟踪算法之间建立类比。生物视觉通过将过去知识与当前视觉输入融合来维持对环境的连续理解。当前人工系统以首帧目标初始化，当对象经历剧烈外观变化、面临竞争性视觉干扰或属于先前未见类别时会遇到困难。作者提出了多种技术来弥合人工视觉与生物视觉之间的差距，重点改善目标辨别、自适应模型更新和空间推理能力。该工作为跟踪研究迈向人类级别感知智能的未来方向提供了发人深省的视角。
